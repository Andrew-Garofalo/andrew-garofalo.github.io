Website Application

Homepage

The user can either click the button named take the medical form examination for now or click the CDL Medical Examination Form tab to go to the form.

 

Create Account

The user will be given a random username and must make a password that satisfies the 4 conditions shown. The conditions will update as the user is typing their custom password and the conditions that must be met are having a password that uses a upper and lower case character, a special character, a number, and must be at least 8 characters long. If the user already has an account then they can just click the login button instead of making a new account.

 

Medical Examination Form Part 1

This is the first section of our form and show the personal information that needs to be filled out.

 

Medical Examination Form Part 2

The second section of our form asks the user to fill out their driver health history.

 

Medical Examination Form Part 3

The last section of our form asks a lot of questions to see if the user has any of the specific condtitions.

 

Results Page

Displays how long the user's certification period is, along with there complete form as a pdf. Also shows any extra wavier forms that the user may need to take to get filled out. Lastly this page shows notes that the user should know about if they want their CDL licence.

 

Form Creator

Idea of this page was to allow the user to scan any new forms to complete in and it will create a form that can be filled out. Unfortunately, due to unforeseen issues we could not finish this. Now this is one of our future project goals.
